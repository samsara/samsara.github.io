Breakthrough Performance.

It'll blow your mind.

Samsara offers break-through real-time performance with the ability to process and index terabytes of data. A small Samsara installation can handle large amount of data and every layer can be scaled separately from the rest of the infrastructure to meet the specific requirements of your business. Samsara is used in large installations with billions of events and over 1/2 million devices connected.

Clients for many languages.

Start publishing events right away.

Samsara provides clients for various languages and platforms allowing you to focus on your functionality. We are always adding new clients, so if you would like a client for the language you use, just drop us a note.

Out of the box deployment templates for the cloud.

You can have your cake and eat it too!

Samsara is designed for the cloud taking advantage of elasticly scalable systems. Samsara provides out-of-the-box deployment templates for Amazon EC2, Amazon ECS and Kubernetes platforms and can be used in non cloud environments as well..
